## Design Direction

Homepage conversion uplift for TPS klimaattechniek: **Daikin-grade polish on the
existing Atmospheric Clarity look** — premium, engineered, trustworthy, conversion-
forward, CWV-safe. Reference world: premium HVAC brand sites (Daikin / Mitsubishi).
All four owner-chosen levers in play (trust & proof · speed-to-contact · 4-pillar
clarity incl. new Warmtepompen · visual wow).

**Notable convergence:** all three sketches independently landed on variant **D** — a
synthesis pairing *conversion mechanics* (proof-forward, inline offerte, frictionless
Bel/WhatsApp) with *engineered Daikin craft* (blueprint texture, top-accent reveal,
live pulse) and *one tasteful interaction* (pillar→form routing · click-to-expand ·
scroll-in sticky bar). That convergence is the uplift's design DNA.

## Key Decisions

- **Tokens:** real MD3 palette (teal `#006580` + green `#006b42`, light surfaces),
  Plus Jakarta Sans / Inter, soft cool-tinted shadows, glass, signature gradient.
- **Hero:** proof bar + engineered inline offerte form (Postcode/Telefoon/Dienst);
  pillar cards route the chosen service into the form; pure-CSS aurora (no WebGL).
- **Pillars:** equal scannable 4-card grid (nothing hidden), brand-logo chips
  (hover-colorize), click-to-expand details. Real taxonomy (4/4/5/4 sub-services).
- **Trust & contact:** proof section (score + reviews + keurmerken + USPs) + a smart
  **site-wide** sticky contact bar (scroll-in, dismissible, mobile safe-area,
  body-level so it's window-fixed).
- **Motion:** fade-ups + CSS aurora only; reduced-motion respected; CWV-safe.
