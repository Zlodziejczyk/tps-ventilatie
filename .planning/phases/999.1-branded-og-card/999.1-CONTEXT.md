# Backlog 999.1: Branded OG / Social-Share Card

**Type:** design task · **Origin:** Phase 3 SEO UAT (gap 12, cosmetic) · **Owner input needed:** logo asset + brand approval

## What

Replace the launch-default `public/og-default.jpg` with a purpose-designed **branded** 1200×630 social-share card.

## Why

`public/og-default.jpg` is the image that renders when the site is shared on WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Facebook, iMessage, etc. The current asset is a **plain grey ventilation-fan product shot on white — no logo, no business name, no tagline** (it was seeded from `public/images/hero-ventilatie.jpg` via `sips` as a deliberate launch-default in plan 03-02; a branded/bespoke card was explicitly deferred).

It's a valid, correctly-sized asset (the SEO wiring around it is done and proven), but as a share card it does nothing for brand recognition or click-through. The owner confirmed during UAT that the card should carry the **TPS klimaattechniek** brand.

## Scope / acceptance criteria

- 1200×630 JPEG at `public/og-default.jpg` (replaces the current file — no code change needed; `OG_IMAGE` already points here and `buildMetadata` references it).
- Carries the **TPS klimaattechniek** brand name/logo.
- Short value-prop tagline, e.g. *airco · warmtepompen · ventilatie · Zoetermeer e.o.* (align with `SITE.tagline` / final copy).
- Brand colors / "Atmospheric Clarity" design language (no #000 black, tonal layering — see CLAUDE.md design system).
- Legible at small render sizes (LinkedIn/WhatsApp thumbnail).
- Re-run the Phase 3 build gate after swap: `out/index.html` `og:image` still resolves; dimensions stay 1200×630 (covered by the spirit of `scripts/assert-seo.ts` + the 03-08 out/ grep gate).

## Dependencies / notes

- Needs the **TPS klimaattechniek logo asset** from the owner (not located in-repo at queue time).
- Natural pairing: **Phase 6 (Homepage conversion uplift)** brand/visual work, or **Phase 4** editorial (final tagline copy). Can also ship standalone — it's a single asset swap.
- Per-pillar bespoke OG cards (airco / warmtepomp / wtw / mv) remain a further enhancement; `node.content.ogImage ?? OG_IMAGE` already supports per-page cards with zero builder change.
